Biography

I am a PostDoc at the National Institute of Informatics, Tokyo. I am interested in almost everything TCS/ Computational Complexity related but my focus is on arithmetic circuit complexity. You can find a list of my current publications in my DBLP or Google Scholar. A more selected view with abstracts can be found on this homepage under Publication. Apart from my main interest in Computational Complexity, I also have many other interest such as programming (as you can see from my github), machine learning,Video Game Making and Music Production. You can find my CV and my software focused CV. I sometimes write posts on either TCS or Games Mechanics.
